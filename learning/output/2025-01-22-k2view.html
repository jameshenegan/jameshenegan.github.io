K2view has been recognized as a Visionary in Gartner’s Magic Quadrant, celebrating its innovative approach to data management through its Micro-Database™ technology. A Micro-Database acts as a “data lake of one,” unifying all data for individual business entities, such as customers or orders, from various source systems.

Key features of the Micro-Database include:

  • Complete and Trustworthy: It continuously syncs with original data sources, ensuring that the data remains current while applying quality and privacy controls.
  • Secure and Scalable: Each Micro-Database is isolated and secured with its own encryption key, minimizing the risk of data breaches. This architecture supports thousands of concurrent queries without contention.
  • Fast and Efficient: Micro-Databases compress data by up to 90%, significantly reducing storage costs and latency. They are cached in memory for rapid access.
  • AI-Ready: Data is accessible in milliseconds, in sync, secured, compliant, distributed, and can be cached or persisted for different use cases.

K2view enables the management of billions of Micro-Databases concurrently using generative data products, providing tools for schema definition, data integration, synchronization rules, transformation logic, and governance policies.

K2view has been recognized as a Visionary in Gartner’s Magic Quadrant, focusing on data tokenization tools that protect personally identifiable information (PII), protected health information (PHI), and other sensitive data by replacing them with non-sensitive tokens. The solution employs a business entity approach, allowing for the secure management of tokens and sensitive data in patented Micro-Database™ structures. This model creates individual “micro-vaults” for each entity (e.g., customers), significantly reducing the risk of mass data breaches compared to a centralized token vault.

Key features of K2view’s data tokenization include:

  1. Enterprise Performance: The platform offers high scalability and can handle numerous tokenization requests with low latency while maintaining data integrity through ACID compliance.

  2. Ingestion from Any Source: It can continuously ingest data from various systems, ensuring PII management occurs without affecting underlying systems.

  3. Maximum Security: Data is encrypted or hashed during transmission and storage, with strict access controls in place. The solution also includes dynamic data masking for additional protection.

  4. Token Lifecycle Management: Tokens can be managed centrally, with features such as format-preserving tokens and scheduled rotation. The system allows for easy restoration of tokenized data in case of loss.

Additional benefits include enhanced data protection through encryption, an intuitive no-code GUI, quick deployment capabilities, and a scalable architecture that accommodates numerous data sources.
